Life Story

You are the only one who knows yourself best. Everyone around you has different things to tell about you, but only you know your true self. There are billions of people on this planet, and every person has a different purpose. Each person finds their mission differently and has a different journey. Thus, when I write about myself, I write about my journey and what makes the person I am because of the trip. I try to be myself, be passionate about my dreams and hobbies, live honestly, and work hard to achieve all that I want to make.

I am a student who lives in Amguri city with my family. All my friends are from different parts of India. Like many people who are residents of the town, my family also has migrated from North-Western India to come and settle here.

Now I’m doing my bachelor’s in the Sikkim Manipal Institute of Technology which is located in Sikkim.

Sikim is a beautiful place. There is greenry everyehere. Mountains and forest form the majority of terrain there. People there have inherited the form of living in the mountains. It is a place well known for its momos and scenry. It is also a tourist hotspot and it serves as a major contributor to the economy of Sikkim.There are limited oppurtinity in the feild of my study there and I plan to complete my bachelors degree and leave Sikkim for the good of myself.
